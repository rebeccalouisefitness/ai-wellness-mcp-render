# Recruit Thank-You Explainer — v3 · 2 minutes max

**Use:** Embedded on `/ai-wellness-business-thank-you` directly under the "Thanks for applying" headline.
**Duration:** 2:00 max. If you're at 2:15 in editing, cut something.
**Audience:** PROSPECTS who just showed interest in AI Wellness — NOT current Herbalife distributors. The recruit page exists to bring NEW people into the team. They have NOT applied (they submitted an interest form). Frame everything for someone considering joining for the first time.
**Voice:** The distributor's own voice (loaded from their Phase 1 brand voice). Calm, direct, peer-to-peer. Not BURN-aggressive. Not coach-y.

**Tokens:**
- `{{DISTRIBUTOR_FIRST_NAME}}` — substituted from profile
- `{{KIT_NAME}}` — substituted from profile (e.g. "The Athlete", "The Mindful")
- `{{CALENDLY_URL}}` — substituted from profile
- `{{DOMAIN}}` — substituted from profile
- `{{DISTRIBUTOR_BRAND_VOICE_STORY}}` — distributor's saved Phase 1 story (woven into the Problem beat — not literal text-replaced)

**Banned phrases:** "queen / boss babe / sis / let's gooo / level up / manifest / journey / blessed / grateful and humbled / 6-figure / passive income / financial freedom / lifestyle of your dreams / get-rich-quick / you just applied".

## SCRIPT (canonical structure — personalize the marked beats, NEVER rewrite the LOCKED beat)

### 0:00 – 0:15 · Hook (personalize)

Open in the distributor's voice. NEVER use "you just applied". Acceptable variants:
- "If you're watching this, you just showed interest in AI Wellness."
- "If you're watching this, you just popped your details in for AI Wellness."
- "If you're watching this, you just dropped your details in to learn about AI Wellness."

Example shape:

> Hey — {{DISTRIBUTOR_FIRST_NAME}} here.
>
> If you're watching this, you just popped your details in and you're curious about AI Wellness. Let me walk you through what it actually is in two minutes.

### 0:15 – 0:45 · The problem (personalize using their `brand_voice_story`)

Weave the distributor's lived experience (from their saved Phase 1 brand voice) into a 3-line setup. Land on "that's another job, not a business" or equivalent pain anchor.

Example shape (using a hypothetical distributor's story):

> Real talk for a second.
>
> [Their own "why I'm doing this" — from `brand_voice_story` / `transformation_story`, in their voice. 2-3 sentences.]
>
> But here's the bit no one tells you: doing it the old way is exhausting. You DM, you post, you follow up, you onboard one person at a time, and the second you stop hustling, the whole thing stops. That's not a business — that's another job.

### 0:45 – 1:30 · The solution (LOCKED — read verbatim, only `{{KIT_NAME}}` substitutes)

🔒 **DO NOT REWRITE THIS BEAT.** This is the canonical AI Wellness pitch. Every recruit-page video must contain all six elements: product name, Claude as business partner, the 11-step system, what it builds (brand voice, two websites, email sequences, ManyChat flows, private community), "{{KIT_NAME}}, in your voice. Not mine.", and the 80/20 outcome.

> AI Wellness fixes that with one stack.
>
> You get Claude — yes, the AI — set up as your business partner, plus an 11-step system that builds you brand voice, two websites, full email sequences, ManyChat flows, and a private community. All wearing your kit, **{{KIT_NAME}}**, in your voice. Not mine.
>
> When you're done, your business runs the way mine does: leads come in through the funnel, AI does the first 80% of every conversation, and you spend your time on the 20% that actually matters — the human stuff.

### 1:30 – 1:50 · Who it's for (personalize)

Filter in the distributor's voice using their `target_customer` mindset from Phase 1.

Example shape:

> Honestly? It's not for everyone. It's for people who want to build a wellness business that actually scales, who'd rather have a system than start from scratch every morning, and who'll put in the reps to make it run.
>
> If you're after get-rich-quick, this isn't it.

### 1:50 – 2:00 · Book the call (personalize)

Sign off in the distributor's voice. Their `signature_phrase` if it fits.

Example shape:

> If that sounds like you, book a 15-minute call with me below the video — no pressure, no weirdness, just two people figuring out if it's a fit.
>
> I'll see you on the call. Let's go.

## How to record this video

### Recording orientation — pick whichever is easier

- **Vertical (9:16)** — record on your phone in portrait. Quickest setup, plays great on mobile. Use Edits (Instagram's free app) so the teleprompter and camera are on the same phone — one-app-and-done.
- **Landscape (16:9)** — phone propped horizontally on a tripod or stack of books at eye level. More "cinematic" on desktop, but requires a second device for the teleprompter or a printed script. Use iPhone's native Camera app or CapCut.

**Recommendation:** Either orientation works. Most distributors do vertical with Edits because it's one-app-and-done. Landscape looks slightly more "business-y" but takes a second device or print-out for the script.

### Framing

- **Frame chest-up, centered.** About a fist of space above your head.
- **Background:** clean wall, plant, or window. No clutter, no Herbalife banners, no gym.

### Lighting

- **Face a window.** Window light is the best free studio lighting in the world.
- Avoid overhead lights — they create raccoon shadows under your eyes.
- Record between 10am and 3pm for soft, even daylight.
