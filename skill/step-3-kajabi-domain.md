## If they ask why Kajabi (and not Wix / Squarespace / ClickFunnels / something else)

> Kajabi is one platform that holds your **website, lead-capture forms, email nurture sequences, and customer tags** — so you don't have to wire five different tools together. You don't sell your products inside Kajabi (those run through your usual ordering channels) — Kajabi is your CRM and follow-up system. It's what every step from here on is built around. The 14-day trial means there's no risk to set it up — if you decide it's not for you, you can cancel before day 14.

## If they already have a Kajabi account

> Even better — sign in and skip ahead to step 3 (registering your domain inside Kajabi). When you're done, just type **"platforms ready."**

## If they already own a domain (e.g. bought through GoDaddy / Namecheap years ago)

> No problem — you can point an existing domain at Kajabi instead of buying a new one. Don't try to set it up alone — once you're in Kajabi, just tell me **"I already own [yourdomain.com]"** and we'll handle the DNS connection together.

## If they're nervous about $71/mo (or $89 if monthly)

> Totally fair. Two things: the **first 14 days are free** so you don't pay until your trial ends, and Kajabi often runs a 50% off promo on your first 12 months — worth checking at signup. If it still doesn't feel right after the trial, you can pause or cancel — no contract.

## If they say "skip kajabi" / want to use a different platform

Gently push back **once**, then respect their call:

> I hear you. If you want to skip Kajabi, the rest of this kit won't work as-is — every later step (your website, your emails, your offers) is built specifically for Kajabi. If you're sure, we can pause here. Want to give the 14-day trial a shot first?

If they still want to skip, reply:

> Got it — let's pause here. Type **"resume"** when you want to keep going. The rest of the kit assumes Kajabi, so I'll do my best to adapt if you're on something else, but it won't be a 1:1 fit.
